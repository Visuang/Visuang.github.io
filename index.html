About Me

Shuang Li

Welcome to my homepage! I am Shuang Li, a Ph.D. student in the Smart Mind and Super Vision Group at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Xinbo Gao and Prof. Jiaxu Leng. My research focuses on human-centered trustworthy video surveillance systems, with an emphasis on ethical AI, privacy-preserving technologies, and reliable visual perception. Previously, I earned my M.Eng. from Kunming University of Science and Technology under the guidance of Prof. Huafeng Li , where I laid the foundation for my current work in intelligent vision systems.

My research aims to bridge the gap between technical robustness and human-centric design in video surveillance, addressing challenges such as fairness, interpretability, and user trust. For details, please explore my publications or contact me directly.
